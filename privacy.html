Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

The Autonomo Tax Calculator ("we", "our", "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Spanish data protection law (LOPDGDD).

1. Data Controller

The data controller for the Service is the operator of the Autonomo Tax Calculator. For privacy inquiries, please contact us through the application.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Offline/Demo Mode (No Account)

We collect no personal data when you use the Service without an account. All information you enter is stored exclusively on your device using browser technologies (IndexedDB and localStorage). We have no access to this data.

2.3 Automatically Collected Data

We do not use cookies for tracking. We do not use third-party analytics services that track individual users. If analytics are implemented, they will be privacy-friendly and aggregate-only (no personal identification).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Sync your data across devices (if you have an account)
  • Send essential service communications (password resets, security alerts)

We do not:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your financial data for advertising or profiling
  • Share your data with tax authorities or government agencies
  • Use your data for automated decision-making

4. Data Storage and Security

Local storage: Data in offline/demo mode is stored on your device only. Clearing your browser data will permanently delete this information.

Cloud storage: Account data is stored on Supabase infrastructure in the EU (eu-west-1, Ireland). Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you clear it or clear your browser data
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • After account deletion: Personal data is deleted within 30 days. Anonymized aggregate data may be retained for service improvement

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR and LOPDGDD, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction — Restrict processing of your personal data
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us through the application. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (Agencia Española de Protección de Datos, AEPD) at www.aepd.es.
